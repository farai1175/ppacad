Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our academy and services.

How do I enroll in a course?

You can enroll in any of our courses by visiting the Courses page, selecting your desired subject and grade level, and clicking the "Enroll Now" button. You'll be guided through a simple registration process.

What is the cost of your courses?

Our course fees vary depending on the subject and grade level. We offer competitive pricing and occasionally run special promotions. Please contact us directly for the most current pricing information.

Do you offer any free trial lessons?

Yes! We offer a free trial lesson for all new students. This allows you to experience our teaching style and platform before committing to a full course. Contact us to schedule your free trial.

What if I need to reschedule a lesson?

We understand that schedules can change. You can reschedule any lesson with at least 24 hours notice through your student portal. For last-minute changes, please contact our support team directly.

Do you provide materials for the courses?

Yes, all necessary learning materials are included with your course enrollment. This includes digital textbooks, practice worksheets, and access to our online resource library.
